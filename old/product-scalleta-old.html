Production Story

Inabel Mastery

The Scalleta requires advanced inabel weaving skills. Our artisans have spent years mastering the tight, precise weaving technique that creates the distinctive pattern and drape. This expertise commands fair compensation that recognizes skill level.

Contemporary Design

While honoring traditional patterns, we've adapted dimensions and finishing for modern use. The size works for professional styling, the weight is travel-friendly, and the edges are finished for durability in everyday wear.

Cultural Heritage

Inabel weaving is a centuries-old Bicolano tradition. By creating market-viable products that showcase this technique, we ensure these skills remain economically relevant and culturally valued for future generations.

Sustainable Production

Upcycled cotton threads reduce textile waste while creating employment in sorting and preparation. The handloom process uses no electricity, making it both environmentally sustainable and resilient to power disruptions.
